Top 5 Recommended Consumer Psychology Books for Marketers

The books give great consumer psychology insights and are relevant to practitioners,

1. Contagious: How to Build Word of Mouth in the Digital Age by Jonah Berger

2. Zero to One: Notes on Startups, or How to Build the Future by Peter Thiel and Blake Masters

3. The Warren Buffett Way: Investment Strategies of the World's Greatest Investor by Robert G. Hagstrom

4. Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion by Robert B. Cialdini

5. Rich Dad Poor Dad: What the Rich Teach Their Kids About Money That the Poor and Middle Class Do Not! by Robert T. Kiyosaki
